About Me

Get to Know Me

Hello, I'm Heya!

I have always had this strong sense of drive to make a difference in the lives of others, especially in view of personal health and wellness. This curiosity has led me to look beyond the surface to discover how we can move forward from treating but cure the auto-immune and threatening diseases that plague the lives of many, so everyone can live the life they deserve.

As I've grown up, I've observed the various healthcare innovations and am determined to accelerate this progress by leveraging biotechnology to solve these problems. I am currently an innovator training at a human accelerator - The Knowledge Society, where I am learning about emerging disciplines like synthetic biology and artificial intelligence and exploring the incredible opportunities that arise when we use them towards their full potentials. I strive to simultaneously live an unconventional life and continue to spend my time doing valuable things that will enable me to 10x my growth, learn alongside driven people, and impact billions. At this moment, I don't know which career direction may take me in, but I am ready to take on and overcome the challenges to build a brighter tomorrow.
